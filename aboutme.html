A few word to introduce myself

My name is Tzach Cohen, a 35-year-old individual happily married to my lovely wife Talia and proud father of three wonderful children: Naveh, Saar and Rom .
I hold a degree in building practical engineering and have been actively working in the field for the past six years. Originally from the Kibbutz Einat, sins 2011 I moved to kibbutz Maagan Michael .
In my free time, one of my greatest passions is music. I love to Listening to a various type of music and artists and also love to playing on guitar aswell.
Apart from music, wave surfing is a true passion of mine. There's something incredibly exhilarating about being out in the water, riding on the waves, and feeling the power of sea. It's a thrilling experience that brings me a sense of freedom.
